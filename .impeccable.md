# Design Context — ArtRevive

## Target Audience
Video designers and projection artists working in creative/live event contexts. Professional users who know their tools — they're not casual, they have taste and high expectations.

## Use Cases
- Transform source images into cinematic projection art
- Generate glowing light sculptures for stage / facade projection
- Create looping animated visuals for live shows, installations, events
- Quickly iterate on world-transform styles for client work

## Brand Personality / Tone
Dark, premium, cinematic studio. Feels like a high-end creative tool — not a consumer app, not generic SaaS. Raw and powerful. The kind of tool that feels good to use at 2am when you're building something for a big show.

## The One Thing
**"The glow effect was insane"** — the UI should make the output feel inevitable and premium. Every interaction should feel like it belongs in a professional creative suite.

## Design Principles
- Dark interface, deep blacks, neon accent colors
- Controls feel like hardware — precise, tactile, no toy-like roundness
- Output is the hero — canvas takes maximum space
- Typography: editorial, confident, not playful
- Motion: purposeful, not decorative
- Gallery-quality framing of generated results
